i What does "Reading Grade" mean?
Reading grade estimates how easy the response is to read, based on sentence length and word complexity. It roughly maps to US school grade levels — a grade 6 response is easy for most people; grade 16+ is academic or technical writing.
Easy
1–6
Like a newspaper or casual blog post. Anyone can read this.
Moderate
7–10
Like a news magazine or high school textbook. Comfortable for most adults.
Complex
11–14
Like a college essay or professional report. Needs some focus to follow.
Expert
15+
Like an academic paper or legal document. Dense and technical.
How similar were the answers?

This compares the content of each pair of tone responses. A high percentage means both tones produced nearly the same answer. A low percentage means the tone meaningfully changed what the AI said.

75–100% Nearly identical — tone had little effect
50–74% Noticeable differences — tone changed the answer somewhat
0–49% Very different — tone had a big impact
