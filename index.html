LLM Tone & Accuracy Tester

Does the tone of a prompt change how an AI responds? This tool empirically tests the hypothesis proposed in Do LLMs Care How You Ask? Prompt Tones and AI Accuracy, Trust, and Engagement (Kotikela et al., ICIS 2025) — sending the same question in four different tones and measuring the differences in response length, complexity, and similarity.
